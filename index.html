About the Festival

Our Mission

CONQuest Festival is the Philippines' premier gaming and esports event, uniting gamers, developers, content creators, and industry leaders for three days of tournaments, exhibitions, and interactive experiences.

Our mission is to celebrate gaming culture by providing a platform where casual and professional gamers can connect, compete, and create unforgettable memories.

Who Should Attend

CONQuest Festival welcomes everyone from the gaming community:

  • Competitive gamers looking to showcase their skills
  • Casual players seeking new gaming experiences
  • Content creators and streamers
  • Game developers and publishers
  • Esports enthusiasts and fans
  • Technology and gaming industry professionals

What to Expect

Experience the best of gaming with:

  • High-stakes esports tournaments with prize pools
  • Exclusive game previews and demos
  • Meet & greet with gaming celebrities and streamers
  • Interactive exhibits and VR experiences
  • Panel discussions and industry insights
  • Cosplay competitions and showcases

Featured Speakers

Mara Aquino

Esports Host & Broadcaster

A dynamic esports host and broadcaster known for her engaging presence and deep industry knowledge. She has hosted major gaming events, bringing excitement and energy to the esports community.

Marla D. Rausch

Founder & CEO, Animation Vertigo

A leader in motion capture technology, Marla has helped bring characters to life in top-tier games. Her company, Animation Vertigo, has played a key role in advancing animation in the gaming industry.

Nikki Roth

Game Designer & Narrative Expert

With years of experience in game design, Nikki specializes in crafting immersive stories and engaging gameplay. She has worked on critically acclaimed titles, shaping memorable gaming experiences.

Venue Information

SM Mall of Asia Complex

The SM Mall of Asia Complex is one of the largest and most iconic event venues in the Philippines, offering world-class facilities and an immersive experience for CONQuest Festival attendees.

The venue features:

  • Expansive convention and exhibition spaces
  • Cutting-edge gaming and esports arenas
  • High-speed internet connectivity throughout the venue
  • Multiple stages for tournaments, panels, and showcases
  • Diverse dining options and food courts
  • VIP lounges and dedicated meeting spaces
  • Easy access to hotels, shopping, and entertainment hubs
  • Convenient public transport and ample parking
